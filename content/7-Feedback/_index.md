> Here you can freely share your personal feedback about the experience of participating in the First Cloud Journey programme, helping the FCJ team improve any remaining shortcomings based on the following categories:

### Overall assessment

**1. Working environment**  
The working environment is very friendly and open. FCJ members are always ready to help when I encounter difficulties, even outside working hours. The workspace is neat and comfortable, which helps me concentrate better. However, I think some extra get‑togethers or team bonding sessions could be added so that people understand each other better.

**2. Support from mentor / admin team**  
The mentor provided very detailed guidance, explained clearly when I didn’t understand something, and always encouraged me to ask questions. The admin team helped with procedures, documents, and created favourable conditions for me to work smoothly. I highly appreciate that the mentor let me try and solve problems on my own instead of just giving the answers.

**3. Relevance of work to study major**  
The work I was assigned is relevant to the knowledge I acquired at university, while also expanding into new areas that I had never been exposed to before. Thanks to this, I was able to reinforce my foundation knowledge while also learning practical skills.

**4. Learning & development opportunities**  
During the internship, I learned many new skills such as using project management tools, teamwork skills, and also how to communicate professionally in a corporate environment. The mentor also shared a lot of practical experience that helped me better orient my career direction.

**5. Culture & team spirit**  
The company culture is very positive: everyone respects each other, works seriously but still happily. When there is an urgent project, everyone works together and helps each other regardless of their position. This made me feel like a part of the team, even though I am just an intern.

**6. Policies / benefits for interns**  
The company provides an internship allowance and offers flexible working hours when needed. In addition, being able to participate in internal training sessions is a big plus.

### Some additional questions

- **What were you most satisfied with during your internship?**  
  I was most satisfied with being assigned a real project (a music streaming website) that lasted 8 weeks, covering everything from interface design and API development to deployment on AWS. This helped me fully understand the product development lifecycle and experience cloud services in a structured way. The mentor’s dedicated guidance and the useful knowledge‑sharing sessions are also things I really treasure.

- **What do you think the company should improve for future interns?**  
  Short training sessions on soft skills such as time management, writing professional reports, or building personal branding could be added. Also, having more opportunities to interact with other intern groups to exchange experiences would be great.

- **If you were to recommend to friends, would you advise them to intern here? Why?**  
  I would definitely recommend it. The First Cloud Journey programme offers a practical learning environment, exposure to in‑demand AWS technologies, and close mentoring. The friendly and open working culture helps interns easily integrate and develop their abilities.

### Suggestions & wishes

- **Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?**  
  I suggest creating a common discussion channel (like Discord/Slack) so that interns can ask quick questions, share materials, and exchange problem‑solving experiences outside of working hours.

- **Would you like to continue with this programme in the future?**  
  I really wish to stay involved with the programme, possibly in a role supporting future cohorts or participating in more advanced projects. If there is an opportunity to become an official intern or collaborator, I am ready to contribute.

- **Other feedback (free sharing):**  
  The programme has done a great job in creating a clear roadmap and specific goals. I hope that in the future, First Cloud Journey will have more in‑depth workshops on security, DevOps, or Machine Learning on AWS so that interns have more options to explore.
