### Areas for improvement

Although many positive results were achieved, I recognise that some aspects can still be further refined for future personal development:

* Continue to enhance personal discipline, especially in strictly adhering to work processes and time management.
* Further develop critical thinking and the ability to solve problems systematically, particularly when facing complex situations.
* Improve professional communication skills, learning to convey ideas more concisely and persuasively in team meetings or reports.
